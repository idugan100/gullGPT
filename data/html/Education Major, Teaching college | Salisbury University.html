Undergraduate Degree Programs

The Seidel School offers the following majors:

  • Early Childhood Education
  • Elementary Education
  • Elementary Education with Dual Certification in Early Childhood Education
  • Outdoor Education Leadership
  • Physical Education

The Seidel School offers Maryland State Department of Education-approved secondary teacher education programs in several majors, including:

  • Biology
  • Chemistry
  • Earth Science
  • English
  • ESOL (English as a Second Language) (*Pre-K 12 certification)
  • French
  • History
  • Mathematics
  • Music (*Pre-K-12 Certification)
  • Physical education (*Pre-K-12 Certification) ( See your advisor for access to the online application)
  • Physics
  • Spanish

Graduate Programs

The Seidel School offers the following graduate programs:

Master’s Degree

  • Curriculum and Instruction (M.Ed.)
  • Curriculum and Instruction (M.Ed.) – Post-Secondary Education Track
  • Educational Leadership (M.Ed.)
  • Mathematics Education (M.S.) - shared with Henson School
  • Reading Specialist (M.Ed.)
  • Teaching (M.A.T.)

Doctoral Degree

  • Contemporary Curriculum Theory and Instruction: Literacy (Ed.D.)

Certificates

  • Post-Baccalaureate Certificate in Higher Education
  • Certificate of Advanced Study in Educational Leadership
  • Post-Master Certificate in Educational Leadership
  • Post-Master Certificate in Literacy Educator
