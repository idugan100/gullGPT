Office of Financial Aid & Scholarships

Your college degree is an investment in your future.  At Salisbury University we are committed to making that investment worthwhile.  As a public Maryland university, we are a powerful combination of value and affordability.   Students have many options to help fund their educational journey here at Salisbury University.  The Office of Financial Aid and Scholarships is here to help guide students and parents through this process.

The Financial Aid Office at Salisbury University is committed to providing you with excellent customer service, timely delivery of information and sensitivity to your individual financial concerns. We are pleased to act as your financial aid resource and information center, and look forward to helping you achieve your educational goals.

Important Financial Aid Announcements

  • 24-25 FAFSA Opening by December 31, 2023

    Due to the passing of the Free Application for Federal Student Aid (FAFSA) Simplification Act on December 27, 2020, as a part of the Consolidated Appropriations Act, the FAFSA is changing for the 2024-2025 academic year. Read more about 24-25 FAFSA Major Changes.

  • Important Information from MHEC

    Important FAFSA Changes from MHEC for Maryland Resident Students: MHEC has extended the Maryland financial aid deadline to submit the 24-25 FAFSA to JUNE 1, 2024. However, students are encouraged to submit the FAFSA by the priority deadline of March 1, 2024 to receive notification of eligibility by April 15, 2024. To learn more about MD State Financial Aid go to Important FAFSA Changes (maryland.gov).

    As soon as you complete and submit your FAFSA or MSFAA, make sure to create a Maryland College Aid Processing System (MDCAPS) Account. This is a critical part of the financial aid process.

    Go to MHEC Maryland website and click on the MDCAPS ICON, which looks like a graduation cap. Please make sure when you sign up that you use an email that you use all the time, because the email you put in as your MDCAPS account is the email we will use to notify you about any scholarships or grants you may be eligible to receive!

  • Get Ready for Student Loan Payments

    For borrowers restarting payments after the COVID-19 payment pause, your first payment is due in October 2023. Take action now to be fully prepared before payment resumes in October. More information can be found at Federal Student Aid - Prepare for Student Loan Payments To Restart.

Feel free to email us your questions at finaid@salisbury.edu or call us at 410-543-6165.
