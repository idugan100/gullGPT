Graduate Research

Salisbury University encourages scholarship and creative activities. SU provides graduate students with an environment of academic excellence through a strong commitment to promote graduate research, education, creativity and faculty-student collaborations.

As a graduate student at SU, you can be actively involved in research under the direction of one of our distinguished faculty. Research Assistantships in various departments offer exciting opportunities to participate in on-going research developments at SU.

Research Opportunities

  • Research and Presentation (RAP) Grant
  • Salisbury University Student Research Conference (SUSRC)
  • Assistantship Openings
  • Find Funding
  • Fulton Student Research Grant
  • Henson Student Research Grant (Contact Lisa Ball for more information)
  • Small Business Development Center (SBDC) "Invest in my Idea" Poster Competition (Contact John Hickman for more information)

Research Compliance

  • Institutional Review Board (IRB)
  • Institutional Animal Care and Use Committee (IACUC)
