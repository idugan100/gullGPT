Orientation Checklist – Students Starting Fall 2023

We are so excited to welcome you to SU and have you become part of the Sea Gull family! Orientation for students starting in Fall 2023 is a multi-step process. Please follow the steps below, designed to help you successfully transition to the Flock!

  • Step 1: Complete your Orientation Registration & Survey

    • Starting in April 2023 you will be able to complete your Orientation Registration & Survey. Completing your Registration Survey will assist your Academic Advisor in creating your first semester schedule. You will receive an email to the personal email address SU has on file when you can access the registration and survey information. 

    • Orientation Registration & Survey will become available in April. Students will receive email communication when it is live. Students will register through their Admissions Status Portal when it is available.

    • Once you submit your orientation registration and survey, you will receive a registration confirmation email containing the information you need to attend your Sea Gull Start-Up Session. 

    The New Student Guide

  • Step 2: Stay up to date with your Admissions Status Portal

    Make sure you are staying up to date with your Admissions Status Portal. Your portal will be updated regularly with many new things for you to do and complete. Some of these include completing your required training before the start of the semester.

  • Step 3: Attend your Sea Gull Start-Up Orientation Session

    • As part of completing your orientation survey and registering for Orientation, students will select a date to attend a Sea Gull Start-Up session. Academic Advising Team members and Orientation Staff members will guide new students and families through an informative session and will make sure all of your questions can be answered. 

    • Unable to make the date you registered for? Click the reschedule button in your confirmation email or email orientation@salisbury.edu

  • Step 4: Complete your Sea Gull Start-Up Online MyClasses Module

    • Beginning in July, you will be added to your Sea Gull Start-Up Online Modules through MyClasses, SU’s online learning platform. Your Sea Gull Start-Up Online modules include five modules that allow for students to learn more about a variety of aspects of SU’s campus life, including housing/residence life, health and wellness services; academic support and managing the finances of your SU education. Students are expected to complete all the modules and pass all the quizzes associated with the modules. 

    • The modules may be accessed through the MyClasses learning platform using your SU username and password to log in on a desktop or mobile device via the link below.

    • Students should complete all modules by August 25

    MyClasses

    • Once logged into MyClasses, students will select the “Sea Gull Start-Up Fall 2023” course box and complete their required online modules on academic advising, campus life, financial aid, and other relevant topics, including a brief overview of their respective academic school. The modules will take approximately 1 ½ hours to complete in all, though students may work through the modules over several saved sessions. 

    • There are 4 additional required training courses that all new students must complete. We expect these trainings (Diversity, Alcoholwise, Consent & Respect, Kognito) will take an additional 2 ½ hours to complete.

    • Students should complete the modules and required trainings prior to August 25th, as all the information provided will be critical for first-semester success at SU. The modules may also continue to be accessed in MyClasses throughout the fall semester as a reference.

    MyClasses

  • Step 5: Join us at First Flight!
    • Join us from August 24-27, 2023 for an in-person experience providing opportunities to connect and meet peers, learn where things are on SU’s campus, find out more about resources and expectations to support your journey and much more!  
  • Step 6: Make sure to pay attention to Fall 2023 Important Dates!
    • 8/28 – First Day of Classes 
    • 9/1 – Regular Session Drop/Add Ends 
    • 11/3 – Regular Session Last Day to Drop a Class (Grade of W) 
    • 11/22-11/26 – Thanksgiving Break 
    • 12/11 – Last Day of Classes 
    • 12/12– Reading Day 
    • 12/13-12/19 – Final Exams 
    • 12/20 – Commencement
