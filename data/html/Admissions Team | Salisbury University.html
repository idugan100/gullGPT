Admissions Team

Selecting the college that is right for you is a very personal – and sometimes challenging – experience. Our team of dedicated admissions professionals is here to make that process easier. We encourage you to introduce yourself via email to the counselor below who assists students from your area. This person can serve as your point of contact through all steps of the admissions process at Salisbury University. We also invite you to visit campus to meet us in person. Your campus visit is the most important step you will take in the whole admissions process, and it really is the only way to know if SU is right for you; it is worth making the trip to our beautiful campus! We look forward to meeting you! For inquiries related to admission to the University, please contact a counselor for your regional territory.
