Student Life

International student scholarships range from USD 3,000 to USD 12,000 and are awarded to new incoming international students only (starting spring 2018 as funds are available). All scholarships are renewable each year (up to 4 years total), subject to satisfactory academic progress.

  • Life at SU

    Beautiful campus, fun activities, and friends for life: You find all of this at SU!

    Are you looking for new ways to show off your creativity?  Do you have a passion for serving the community? By getting involved, you can learn new skills, challenge yourself, develop leadership qualities, meet new people, and enrich your university experience.

    At SU, it’s about more than just the classroom.  For instance, do you like sports?  We have several sports groups that aren’t represented by the SU athletic teams.

    Are you interested in business or politics?  Join our organizations for Entrepreneurs, the Student Government Association, or many others!

    If you’re an international student, you’ll find groups that help you connect with communities on campus!

    At SU, you’ll also find:

    • Cultural Activities
    • Greek Society (Sororities and fraternities)
    • Year-round Campus Events
    • And much, much more!!!

    Explore the more ways to get involved by visiting the Center for Student Involvement & Leadership.

  • Housing

    Come on in and make yourself at home!  University Housing is convenient, safe, outfitted, and affordable.

    On campus housing is a great opportunity to make lasting friendships and share new learning experiences.  All first and second year students are guaranteed housing at SU.  As a resident student you’ll choose from traditional residence halls or on-campus apartment options, all of which feature:

    • Basic furniture
    • High speed wireless internet access
    • Free laundry facilities
    • A hall kitchen
    • Cable television

    Your rooms are within walking distance of classrooms, facilities and labs, dining halls, convenience stores and groceries, sports and entertainment venues, and transportation.

    After you have been admitted and confirmed your enrollment, international freshmen students are assigned housing through University Housing.

  • Dining

    Salisbury’s Dining Services offers award-winning chefs on the cutting-edge of the culinary arts.  SU offers your taste buds variety!

    • The Market Place and Bistro Café (buffet style – all you can eat)
    • Chick-fil-A
    • Coffee, sandwiches and pastries at SU Café’s
    • Quick grab & go options

    Meal plans are available for any type of lifestyle. Moreover, there are many restaurants within walking distance of campus, as well as a Starbucks and grocery stores.

  • What's New for You?

    In addition to SU’s beautiful grounds, there are a collection of state-of-the-art buildings designed to help you excel.  SU enhanced academics and athletics with two new facilities in 2016.  The Guerrieri Academic Commons revolutionizes how you think about libraries, and the new Sea Gull Stadium is among the best complexes in NCAA Division III.
